About Me

My name is Eric Grant and this is my website. I recently graduated from Seattle Pacific University with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science. While studying at Seattle Pacific University, I was the financial officer for the Seattle Pacific University Developer's Club. My role as the financial officer was to help plan and organize events, meetings, and activities for the club members throughout the year. This included events such as Hackathons, company tours, and weekly programming sessions.
